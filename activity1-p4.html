Pork Adobo

Home About Us Contact Us Food Menu Recipes References

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg pork belly, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 cup water

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, combine pork, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, peppercorns, and bay leaves. Marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer.
  3. Add water and cook until pork is tender and sauce has thickened.
