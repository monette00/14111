Chicken Inasal

Home About Us Contact Us Food Menu Recipes References

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken thighs
  • 1/4 cup calamansi juice
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp annatto oil
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken in calamansi juice, soy sauce, garlic, salt, and pepper for 2 hours.
  2. Grill chicken while basting with annatto oil.
  3. Serve hot with rice and dipping sauce.
