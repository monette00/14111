Beef Kaldereta

Home About Us Contact Us Food Menu Recipes References

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg beef (chuck, brisket, or shank)
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 medium potatoes, peeled and sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1 can (200g) tomato paste
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup liver spread or paté
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup green peas (optional)
  • 1 cup grated cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Brown the beef in batches and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, sauté onions and garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato paste and cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Return the beef to the pot. Add soy sauce, vinegar, and beef broth. Stir in the bay leaf and dried thyme. Bring to a simmer.
  5. Cover and cook for about 1-1.5 hours or until the beef is tender.
  6. Add potatoes, carrots, and bell peppers. Continue cooking until the vegetables are tender.
  7. Stir in liver spread or paté for richness and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.
  8. Optional: Add green peas and cheese, and let them simmer until the peas are tender and the cheese melts.
  9. Serve hot with steamed rice.
