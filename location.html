Visit Us At:

Address:

335 Brgy. Buenglat, San Carlos City, Pangasinan
Zip Code: 2420

Directions:

  • From the city center of San Carlos, head north towards Brgy. Buenglat.
  • Pass by the San Carlos City Hall, and continue straight.
  • Turn right when you reach the main junction, and continue along the road.
  • The restaurant will be on your left side, just before you reach the local market.

Landmarks:

  • San Carlos City Hall – The restaurant is located near this landmark.
  • Local Market – A well-known spot close to the restaurant.
