Pancit Canton

Home About Us Contact Us Food Menu Recipes References

Ingredients:

  • 500g Pancit Canton noodles
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 lb pork (sliced into thin strips)
  • 1/2 lb chicken breast (sliced into thin strips)
  • 1/2 cup shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • 1/2 cup sliced carrots
  • 1/2 cup snow peas
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers (sliced)
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped green onions for garnish
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Pancit Canton noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan or wok, heat oil over medium heat. Saut√© onions and garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add pork and chicken, cooking until browned. Add shrimp and cook until pink.
  4. Add carrots, snow peas, and bell peppers, stir-frying for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Pour in chicken broth, soy sauce, and oyster sauce. Stir to combine and bring to a simmer.
  6. Add the cooked Pancit Canton noodles, tossing to coat in the sauce. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until the noodles are well combined and heated through.
  8. Garnish with green onions and serve with lemon wedges.
