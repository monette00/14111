Sinigang na Baboy

A classic Filipino sour soup dish

Home About Us Contact Us Location Recipes References

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg pork ribs
  • 1 packet sinigang mix
  • 1 medium tomato, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 1 cup kangkong leaves
  • 2 pcs gabi (taro), peeled and cubed
  • 5 cups water

Instructions:

  1. Boil pork with water, tomato, and onion until tender.
  2. Add gabi and cook until soft.
  3. Mix sinigang powder, kangkong leaves, and simmer for 2 minutes.
